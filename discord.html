READ THE RULES ☾

Don’t be a problem

Respect people. Respect identities. Respect boundaries. Harassment, hate speech, or bullying = instant nope.

Safe Space, Not a Debate Club

Different opinions are fine. Invalidating, mocking, or dogpiling people is not.

NSFW Content is Role-Gated

Memes, ship art, and suggestive content stay in the 18+ section. Access is not automatic. Request the role from a mod.

  • Absolutely no NSFW involving minors. Instant ban.
  • No NSFW outside designated channels. No exceptions.

No Illegal or Harmful Content

No doxxing, malware, or real-world threats. Keep it legal, keep it safe.

Keep it Usable

Put things in the right channels. If you’re unsure, ask a mod instead of guessing.

Anonymous Sharing

Sharing work anonymously is allowed. Pressuring someone to reveal themselves is not cool.

No Spam / Promo Dumping

Sharing your work is encouraged; spamming links and begging for attention isn't.

Lurking is Valid

You don’t have to talk or post to be welcome here. Just exist and enjoy.

☾ Final Note

This server is meant to feel open, creative, and a little messy — not hostile or unsafe. Use common sense. Be chill. Create freely.
